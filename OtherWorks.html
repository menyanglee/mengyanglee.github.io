Introduction

In the 3D modeling section, I completed an independent project. The project focused on form design and spatial construction, where I used Blender to create an architectural model with complex geometric structures.The experiences allowed me to master the 3D modeling workflow and develop problem-solving skills for complex design challenges.

In the UI interface design section, I designed a mobile application interface with a focus on user experience and interaction logic. Through user research, wireframing, and visual refinement, I completed the entire design process from concept to high-fidelity prototypes. This project deepened my understanding of balancing user needs with design aesthetics and enhanced my proficiency in Figma and Adobe XD.

Through these projects, I not only mastered various design tools and techniques but also cultivated a cross-disciplinary design mindset. These experiences have laid a solid foundation for my future career and ignited my passion for the diversity and possibilities within the design field.
