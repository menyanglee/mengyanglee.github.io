AirFood

Introduction

This project explores innovative research on food, focusing on how programmable control of food can create personalized experiences for users. We are investigating how technology can be used to alter the form, texture, or flavor of food to deliver unique sensory experiences. The project is expected to be completed by May 2024, and the findings will be submitted to a relevant academic journal.

As the project is still ongoing, further details are not yet publicly available. If you are interested in learning more, feel free to reach out to me via email.
